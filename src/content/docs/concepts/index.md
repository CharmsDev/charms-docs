The goal of this section is to explain **what the Charms protocol is** and
**why it exists** — programmable assets on Bitcoin (and beyond), the problems
it was built to solve, and how the pieces fit together.

If you want steps to follow to complete a task, see the [How-to guides](/how-to); if you need exact formats and APIs, see the [Reference](/reference).

- **[Why Charms?](/concepts/why-charms)** — the problem Charms solves and how
  it compares to other Bitcoin token protocols.
- **[Apps](/concepts/apps)** — everything in Charms is an app; what an app
  contract is and how it governs assets.
- **[Spells](/concepts/spells)** — the metadata that creates and transforms
  charms, and what makes a spell *correct*.
- **[Transactions](/concepts/transactions)** — how spells and their proofs
  are embedded in Bitcoin and Cardano transactions.
- **[Beaming](/concepts/beaming)** — moving charms across chains without a
  custodial bridge.
- **[Scrolls](/concepts/scrolls)** — Internet Computer canisters that act as
  programmable, keyless signers for Charms.
